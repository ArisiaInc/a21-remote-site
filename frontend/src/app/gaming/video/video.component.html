Welcome to the Video Game Room!

We’re running a Minecraft server! It’s staffed with helpers 10am-10pm (hours subject to change). The server address is minecraft.arisia.org.
To join an external server, follow the instructions on this Minecraft wiki page.

Throughout the weekend, we will run sessions of various Jackbox games and also Among Us for the community to participate in. They will run on Zoom. The links are posted in the schedule.

Want to play another game with your friends from Arisia? Use the #lfg-video-games channel on Discord to search for a group. Game ratings allowed: E, E10+, T

Keep in mind that on the Minecraft server, in community games, and in pickup games, the Arisia Code Of Conduct applies. This means that if you participate in griefing or trolling, Arisia will treat that behavior as if it happened in person, at the convention. Be nice.
