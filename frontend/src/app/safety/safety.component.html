SAFETY

The Watch aims to be a group you can come to in any situation you are concerned about to be a safe person to help. This continues to be our main purpose for the online convention this year. We are here to provide support for issues of cyber bullying and online fraud that arise in our remote spaces.

If you need to talk to a Safety staff member, you can do so on Discord in the #safety channel or email safety@arisia.org.

To report a Code of Conduct violation or other member behavior issues, contact the IRT.
