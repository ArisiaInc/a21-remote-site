INCIDENT REPORT TEAM

The IRT is specifically tasked with intaking and addressing any reports of violation of the Code of Conduct or other member behavior issues. At any time, you can:
File a report using the online form.
File a report at incidents@arisia.org.

You can also speak with an IRT staff member on Discord in the #irt-hall-desk channel during the following hours:
Friday 6pm-10pm EST
Saturday 10am-6pm EST
Sunday 10am-6pm EST
Monday 10am-3pm EST

More information about what to expect when reporting is available here.

To get help with other issues or concerns, contact Safety.
